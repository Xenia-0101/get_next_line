1* What I need is a sheep. Draw me a sheep."
2* So then I made a drawing.
3* He looked at it carefully, then he said:
4* "No. This sheep is already very sickly. Make me another."
5* So I made another drawing.
6* My friend smiled gently and indulgently.
7* "You see yourself," he said, "that this is not a sheep. This is a ram. It has horns."
8* So then I did my drawing over once more.
